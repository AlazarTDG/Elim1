Mini-MBA Programs

Mini-MBA Business Administration

Mini-MBA in Business Administration program is designed to enhance your business skills through an intensive and fast-paced online course. The program covers all the fundamental areas of general business management, providing you with the necessary knowledge and tools to succeed in your career.

Learn more

Mini-MBA Information Technology Program

Mini-MBA Information Technology Program is a comprehensive program designed to provide participants with a strong foundation in essential management skills, human resources management, innovation management, project management, marketing essentials, and IT management.

Learn more

Mini-MBA in Leadership Program

Mini-MBA leadership program is designed to equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to effectively lead teams, manage organizations, drive innovation, and navigate change in a dynamic business environment. 

Learn more

Mini-MBA Project Management

Mini-MBA in Project Management program is designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to manage projects effectively. The program covers the full project management lifecycle, from initiation to closure, and provides students with a deep understanding of the principles, tools, and techniques used in the field.

Learn more

Mini-MBA Innovation Management

Mini-MBA in Innovation Management Program is a comprehensive program designed to provide participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to manage innovation within their organization. The program covers a range of topics related to innovation, including ideation, design thinking, technology management, and commercialization.

learn more

Mini-MBA in Global Governance

Mini-MBA in Global Governance will provide an in-depth exploration of the concept of global governance and its relevance in the current global landscape. It will examine the various actors and institutions involved in global governance and the challenges and opportunities they face. Program will also analyze the evolving dynamics of global governance in light of the changing balance of power and the emergence of new global actors.

Learn more

Mini-MBA in Financial Management

Mini-MBA Financial Management Program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of financial concepts, accounting principles, investment fundamentals, economics, and international business. 

Learn more

Mini-MBA Strategic Management

Mini-MBA in Strategic Management Program is designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to develop and implement effective strategic plans. 

Learn more
